About Numeris

Operating since 1944, Numeris is Canada's most trusted and authoritative source for broadcast measurement and consumer behavior data, as well as the industry-leading intelligence provider to broadcasters, advertisers and agencies. By providing business-critical insights, we enable our members to deliver relevant, high-quality programming that meets the needs and wants of Canadians.

To learn more about Numeris, please visit our website at numeris.ca.

About the Numeris Archives

The Numeris Archives include approximately 6,500 books published from 1944 to 2009, which contain radio and television audience data for numerous Canadian markets. Originally only available to Numeris members, this material has been made accessible to the public in an effort to preserve Canada’s broadcast history.
