Notice Regarding Senate Debates Availability

Only Senate debates available in both English and French are included on the Canadian Parliamentary Historical Resources portal. The Senate debates from the 2nd through 5th sessions of the 3rd Parliament, as well as all the debates for the 4th to 7th Parliaments, covering the 1875 to 1896 period, do not exist in French because they were never published in that language by the Senate. The Senate's English debates from 1875 to 1896 may be found in other online databases or in libraries across Canada. For more information, contact the Parliament of Canada Information Service or the Senate of Canada.
